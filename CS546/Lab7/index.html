About Jason Rossi

Biography

I come from a middle to upper class family, where I went to public school up until high school. I then went ot Delbarton School for high school where I learned and grew as a person. In my free time, I enjoy coding, playing soccer, poker, golf, chess, and spending time with family and friends. I also spend a great deal of my time following the stock market and keeping up to date with how current stocks are performing such as Apple, Tesla, and Roku to name a few.

When it comes to how I got into Computer Science, my route was not very standard. Growing up, I had my heart set on finance. My oldest brother got a job working in finance and then I saw how often he had to work. He asked me if I wanted to work a third of the time for half of the pay that he gets and it was a no brainer. He said to go into Computer Science and I would never have to worry about getting a job and making a living. I took this to heart and proceeded to drop out of the AP Macroeconomics course that I had signed up for and replaced it with an AP Comptuer Science course. This turned out to be the best decision I had ever made for my life moving forward as I now really enjoy Comptuer Science and learning about the endless possibilities it has to offer.
